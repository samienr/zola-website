Hi, I’m Samien.
I like making cool things.

I’m currently working on my Bachelor’s degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin, where I am also an undergraduate research assistant in Dan Wasserman’s Mid-IR Photonics Lab. As of now, I am interested in computer architecture, RF, and signal processing.

I enjoy photography and designing+building ergonomic keyboards, and also recently started tinkering with a homelab server.

When I have a little free time, I like rock climbing, watching films, and making digital art.

When I have A LOT of free time, I often work on animations that explain mathematical or engineering concepts using Grant Sanderson’s (3Blue1Brown) manim Python library and Adobe After Effects. I also recently started trying to learn blender.

I started this website to share whatever I learn with the world. Hopefully it offers benefit to someone!

If you have any inquiries or would just like to say hello, feel free to reach out! I’d love to hear from you.
