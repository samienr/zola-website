About

Hi, I'm Samien.
I like breaking making things.

I'm currently working on my Bachelor's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering.

I am currently interested in computer architecture, RF, and signal processing.

I enjoy photography, designing+building ergonomic keyboards, and maintaining my homelab. I also started looking into reverse engineering and hardware hacking, which I've been having a lot of fun with.

When I have a little free time, I like rock climbing, watching films, and making digital art.

When I have A LOT of free time, I often work on animations that explain mathematical or engineering concepts using Grant Sanderson's (3Blue1Brown) manim Python library and Adobe After Effects.

I started this website to share whatever I learn with the world. Hopefully it offers benefit to someone!

If you have any inquiries or would just like to say hello, feel free to reach out! I'd love to hear from you.
